Instructor-Provided Evaluation Questions
=========================================

Q1. Why does Macbeth kill Duncan?
Q2. Who is Hamlet?
Q3. What is the conflict between the Montagues and the Capulets?
Q4. Why does Hamlet delay taking revenge?
Q5. Generate a short Shakespearean-style response from Juliet explaining her conflict after meeting Romeo.

Group-Designed Evaluation Questions
=====================================

G1. What role do the witches play in Macbeth?
G2. How does Lady Macbeth influence Macbeth's decision to kill Duncan?
G3. What happens to Ophelia and why?
G4. Why do Romeo and Juliet keep their marriage a secret?
G5. Generate a short Shakespearean-style speech from Macbeth after he decides to kill Duncan.

Notes
=====
- Questions Q1-Q5 are instructor-provided and shared across all groups.
- Questions G1-G5 are group-designed to test additional system capabilities:
    G1: retrieval of a recurring thematic element (witches across multiple scenes)
    G2: multi-scene contextual reasoning for a single motivation
    G3: cross-scene narrative coherence (Ophelia's arc across Acts 3-4)
    G4: grounding in Romeo and Juliet's feud context
    G5: stylised generation on a different play from Q5
- Stylised questions (Q5, G5) are scored on style_quality in addition to usefulness.
- Full responses and scores are in results/evaluation_results.csv.
